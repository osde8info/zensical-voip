[Torkii](http://www.torkii.com/ "Torkii") _is a free service that allows you to record a short voice message and share it with your   friends via email, Twitter or Facebook. Your messages can be up to 30 seconds long and we’ll keep them on our servers for 30 days. Talk, to anyone, for free. It's as simple as that._
