[http://emeadev.blogspot.com/2009/09/raw-audio-manipulation-in-android.html](http://emeadev.blogspot.com/2009/09/raw-audio-manipulation-in-android.html "android")

[Version 1.5](http://developer.android.com/sdk/android-1.5.html "Version 1.5") of the Android SDK introduced a bunch of cool new features for developers.

_Though not as glamorous as some APIs, the new audio manipulation classes -_ [_AudioTrack_](http://developer.android.com/reference/android/media/AudioTrack.html "AudioTrack") _and_ [_AudioRecord_](http://developer.android.com/reference/android/media/AudioRecord.html "AudioRecord") _- offer powerful functionality to developers looking to manipulate raw audio._
