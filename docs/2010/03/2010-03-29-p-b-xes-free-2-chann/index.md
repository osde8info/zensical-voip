The Android App SIPdroid recommends you access your SIP account(s) via a [pbxes](https://www1.pbxes.com/iptel_virtual-pbx.html "pbxes") PBX. It turns out that as well as a hosted multi-channel Asterisk PBXs [pbxes](https://www1.pbxes.com/iptel_virtual-pbx.html "pbxes") also offer a [FREE 2 channel hosted PBX](https://www1.pbxes.com/iptel_virtual-pbx.html "pbxes") with a user friendly web gui that manages the Asterisk .CONF files for you !
