@vodafone will start switching off 3G next year

- https://www.cityam.com/the-future-is-4g-and-5g-vodafone-gears-up-for-3g-switch-off/

_Retiring 3G, after 17 years and nearly 500 billion minutes of calls, is a key part of Vodafone’s strategy to give customers the most reliable network experience. Today, less than four per cent of the data used on Vodafone’s network travels on 3G, in comparison to more than 30 per cent in 2016._
