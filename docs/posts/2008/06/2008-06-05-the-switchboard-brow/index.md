[the switchboard](http://theswitchboard.ca "the switchboard") browser / Java based softphone only uses your internet connection and CPU when you are placing a call and their revenue streams will consist of advertising on the site, and licensing to other web sites, not utilizing your computer as a resource for other customers.

[the switchboard](http://theswitchboard.ca "the switchboard") _will always be free for the individual user._
