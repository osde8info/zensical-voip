---
title: "Tesco is closing its VOIP Internet Telephone service"
date: 2010-03-26
categories: 
  - "voippix"
---

Tesco will be shutting down its VOIP service from 27th April 2010. They will no longer be providing Tesco internet phone and Talk Wi-fi services.

_From 27th April 2010 your Tesco internet phone account will be closed and the Tesco internet phone website will not be available. You will no longer be able to access your online account or be able to make calls using your Tesco internet phone._
