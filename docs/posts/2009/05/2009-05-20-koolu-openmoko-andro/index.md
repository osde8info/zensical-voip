[Koolu](http://koolu.com/ "koolu") can help you gain access to open telephony, where you can chose your phone operating system and phone applications, because it _buys and distributes open phones such as the [Openmoko NEO FreeRunner](http://www.openmoko.com/index.html "openmoko"), acting as a master distributor_. [Koolu](http://koolu.com/ "koolu") also provides [Google Android for the FreeRunner](http://freerunner.android.koolu.com/ "koolu") and their are also some [OpenKoolu](http://www.youtube.com/user/openkoolu "openkoolu") Videos on YouTube !
