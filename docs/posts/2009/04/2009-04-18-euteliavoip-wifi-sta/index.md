[EuteliaVoip WiFi starter kit](http://wifi.euteliavoip.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=8&Itemid=2&lang=en "euteliavoip")

This starter KIT is perfect for those who want to navigate in internet and make phone calls. The Starter Kit costs 199 Euro (VAT included)(\*) and includes a Wi-Fi router to connect to Eutelia WiFi network and a phone adapter (ATA). The phone adapter has been conceived for small offices and firms, but its flexibility makes it perfect for residential use. It is indeed a very cheap gateway with remarkable performances. It has two FXS where you can connect two telephones or 2 faxes with different numbers.
