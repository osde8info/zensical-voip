Digium's [Fax For Asterisk](http://www.digium.com/en/products/software/faxforasterisk.php "asterisk") is _a commercial facsimile (Fax) termination and origination solution designed to enhance the capabilities of Open Source and commercial Asterisk as well as Switchvox. Fax For Asterisk bundles a suite of user-friendly Asterisk applications and a licensed version of the industry's leading fax modem software from Commetrex._
