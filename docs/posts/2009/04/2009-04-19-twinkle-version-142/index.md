[Twinkle Version 1.4.2](http://www.xs4all.nl/~mfnboer/twinkle/index.html "twinkle") SIP softphone was released on 25 Feb 2009

See [news](http://www.xs4all.nl/%7Emfnboer/twinkle/news.html) for a detailed listing of the changes. This version gives you easy integration with [Diamondcard Worldwide Communication Service](http://www.diamondcard.us/?twinkle) for making worldwide calls to regular and cell phones and sending SMS messages.
