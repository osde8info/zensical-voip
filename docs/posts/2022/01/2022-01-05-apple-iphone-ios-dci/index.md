in apple iphone ios 12 DCIM subdirs used to be named

- nnnAPPLE
- nnnCLOUD

in apple iphone ios 15 DCIM subdirs are now named

- yyyymm\_x
- yyyymm\_y

where x is "\_" and y is "a"
