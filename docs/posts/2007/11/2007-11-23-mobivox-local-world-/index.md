#### [MOBIVOX](http://www.mobivox.com/about/ "mobivox") makes it possible to call family and friends around the world for a local call from ANY phone. Just call a local MOBIVOX number and say who you'd like to speak with. No calling cards and long numbers to remember. No need for a computer, special software or complex downloads.
