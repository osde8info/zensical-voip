[Yeigo](http://www.yeigo.com/ "yeigo") is an innovative application for your mobile phone which uses your phones internet connection, (dont worry, your phone has one) to offer you [cut-price](http://www.yeigo.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=38&Itemid=73) calls and SMS messages, and free instant messaging.

Got something to say? Talk whenever you want, for as long as you want: calls to other people using Yeigo are [free](http://www.yeigo.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=38&Itemid=73), and youll save up to 80% on calls to those who dont have Yeigo. \[[Yeigo Rates](http://www.yeigo.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=24&Itemid=43)\]

Not in the mood to talk? Chat to other Yeigo users for free, as well as users of GoogleTalk, MSN, Yahoo!, AIM, ICQ and Jabber - right from your mobile phone. Also send SMS messages to anyone - for up to 90% less. \[[Yeigo Rates](http://www.yeigo.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=24&Itemid=43)\]

Make calls, chat and send SMSs to anyone, anywhere in the world quickly and easily whether you're on the go, in the office, or relaxing at home.
