## Call over 2,000 VoIP networks for free!

[SIP Broker](http://www.sipbroker.com/sipbroker/action/login "sipbroker") is a free service that provides unlimited VoIP calls to millions of people around the world. No signup is necessary.

**How Does It Work?**

Simply find the SIP-Code for the network you wish to reach in our [Network Directory](http://www.sipbroker.com/sipbroker/action/providerWhitePages) and enter it into your phone followed by your friend's phone number. Your call will be connected, free of charge!
