[iNum](http://www.inum.net/what-is-inum/what-is-inum/ "inum") stands for international number - its a new global / country / world dialing code !

iNum _is a new kind of global phone number for a new kind of world -- a world with a new geography that's about local presence and global relationships, not about distance or national borders. iNum is making use of the +883 global country code newly created by the ITU (International Telecommunication Union)._

You can get a free iNum from [http://www.callcentric.com/](http://www.callcentric.com/support/device/other "callcentric")

1. Sign up @ [https://www.callcentric.com/login/](https://www.callcentric.com/login/ "callcentric")
2. Click registration email link
3. Select inum from the 'country' list on [http://www.callcentric.com/coverage/international\_phone\_number](http://www.callcentric.com/coverage/international_phone_number "callcentric")
4. Set up your VOIP phone as per [http://www.callcentric.com/support/device/other](http://www.callcentric.com/support/device/other "callcentric")
5. Call your iNum from a iNum partner or a dial-in number [http://www.inum.net/what-is-inum/inum-partners/](http://www.inum.net/what-is-inum/inum-partners/ "inum")

Have fun !
