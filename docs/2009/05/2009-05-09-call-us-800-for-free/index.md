Howto call, dial or phone US 800 & 888 & 1-800 & webinar dial-in freephone & toll-free numbers for free from anywhere or for the price of a local call from a UK landline telephone or a UK mobile phone using the power of VOIP !

For FREE using [Ubuntu](http://www.ubuntu.com/ "ubuntu"):

1. Get [Ubuntu](http://www.ubuntu.com/ "ubuntu")
2. Get a VOIP softphone ([Ekiga](https://www.ekiga.net/ "ekiga"), Gizmo, etc)
3. Get a VOIP SIP account ([Ekiga](https://www.ekiga.net/ "ekiga"), [CallCentric](http://www.callcentric.com/ "callcentric"), etc)
4. Connect to [SIPBroker](http://sipbroker.com/sipbroker/action/providerWhitePages "sipbroker") 800 service by dialing \*1 on Ekiga (\*275\*1 on CallCentric)
5. Dial the 800/888 number
6. Example : \* 1 800 466 4411

For the price of a LocalCall (or FREE if you have any inclusive UK minutes left !):

1. Dial into a [SIPBroker](http://sipbroker.com/sipbroker/action/providerWhitePages "sipbroker") gateway from any UK landline or UK mobile
2. Connect to [SIPBroker](http://sipbroker.com/sipbroker/action/providerWhitePages "sipbroker") 800 service by dialing \*1
3. Dial the 800 or 888 number followed by #
4. Example : 020 7100 6747 \* 1 800 466 4411 #
