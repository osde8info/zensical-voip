[SIP Communicator](http://www.sip-communicator.org/index.php "sip-communicator") is an audio/video Internet phone and instant messenger that supports some of the most popular instant messaging and telephony protocols such as SIP, Jabber, AIM/ICQ, MSN, Yahoo! Messenger, Bonjour, IRC, RSS and soon others like IAX.

SIP Communicator is completely [Open Source](http://www.opensource.org/) / [Free Software](http://www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw.html), and is freely available under the terms of the [GNU Lesser General Public License](http://www.gnu.org/licenses/lgpl.html).
