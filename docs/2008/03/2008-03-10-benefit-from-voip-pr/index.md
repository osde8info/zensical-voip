There a number of providers let you dial a **084** or **087** number (from the UK) and redirect your call to an international / overseas number for you !

For example with [www.phonecheap.co.uk](http://www.phonecheap.co.uk/index2.php "phonecheap") you can dial **084 4300 4242** to call landlines or dial **087 1551 6868** to call mobiles in Turkey !

You can find lists of providers at [www.magsys.co.uk](http://www.magsys.co.uk/telecom/opnotes.htm "magsys") or [simply dial providers](http://callchecker.moneysavingexpert.com/intcallchecker/#providers "money saving expert") and there are more international / overseas voip telephone calling tips at [www.moneysavingexpert.com](http://www.moneysavingexpert.com/phones/cheap-overseas-calls "money saving expert")
