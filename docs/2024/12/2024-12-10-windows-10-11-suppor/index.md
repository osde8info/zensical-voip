Wi-Fi 6

_Wi-Fi 6, also known as IEEE 802.11ax High Efficiency (HE), is the sixth generation of the Wi-Fi standard. It brings several enhancements to wireless networks (WLANs) and is designed to improve performance, efficiency, and scalability._

Wi-Fi 7

_Wi-Fi 7, also known as IEEE 802.11be Extremely High Throughput (EHT), is the latest Wi-Fi technology that offers unprecedented speed, reliability, and efficiency for your wireless devices._
