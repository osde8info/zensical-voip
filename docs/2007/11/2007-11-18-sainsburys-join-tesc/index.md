Sainsbury's have just joined [Tesco VOIP](http://www.tescointernetphone.com/ "tesco") and M&S GSM telcos.

Sainsbury's are currently undercutting Tesco USB VOIP phone by £1 but are using the same [Freshtel](http://www.freshtelinternetphone.co.uk/ "frehtel") / [Voicedot](http://www.voicedot.com/ "voicedot") alliance infrastructure so that inter Sainsbury's VOIP 2 Tesco VOIP and Tesco VOIP 2 Sainsbury's VOIP calls are FREE !

Sainsbury's and Tesco VOIP customers can also talk for FREE to [Woolworths VOIP](http://www.woolworths.co.uk/web/jsp/product/index.jsp?pid=50759004 "woolworths") phones  !
