how to transfer your mobile number to three and use london underground wifi

- http://www.three.co.uk/support/switching/switch-to-three/details
- https://www.three.co.uk/support/switching/switch-to-three/pac-request

setup a three mobile tube password then

_When you're next in a London Underground station with Wi-Fi:_

_Turn on Wi-Fi on your device._

_Search for Virgin Media WiFi from the available connections and connect to it._ _On the Virgin Media page that opens in your browser, go to the Other providers dropdown and select Three._

_Enter your Three number and the password you chose, then select Register._

_You'll now be able to connect to Wi-Fi in London Underground stations. Your device will remember your details in future, so you'll automatically connect next time you're at a participating underground station._
