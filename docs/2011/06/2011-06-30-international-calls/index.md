If you have a UK landline try just [http://www.just-dial.com](http://www.just-dial.com/)

- first call [http://www.just-dial.com](http://www.just-dial.com/) (084 4720 0990 for landlines in Turkey) before your international number and pay ~1p
- first call [http://www.just-dial.com](http://www.just-dial.com/) (084 4904 0990 for mobiles in Turkey) before your international number and pay ~4p

If you have a UK [three](http://www.three.co.uk/ "three") mobile phone try [http://www.dialacode.com](http://www.dialacode.com/)

- dial the [http://www.dialacode.com](http://www.dialacode.com/) prefix (290429 for landlines in Turkey) before your international number and pay ~5p
- dial the [http://www.dialacode.com](http://www.dialacode.com/)  prefix (290729 for mobiles in Turkey) before your international number and pay ~8p

Note : These prefixs sometimes change so check [http://www.just-dial.com](http://www.just-dial.com/) and [http://www.dialacode.com](http://www.dialacode.com/) sites for the correct prefix for your international destination and for latest prefix to use
