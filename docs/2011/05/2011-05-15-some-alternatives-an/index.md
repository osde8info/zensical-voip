here is a shortlist of some of the many alternatives and replacements that are available for m$ skype

### "all-in-one" mobile solutions

[fring](http://www.fring.com/ "fring") + 4 way video over 3G [tango](http://tango.me/ "tango") + video over 3G

[viber](http://www.viber.com/) android and ios voip app

### voip sip mobile softphones

[android](http://developer.android.com/reference/android/net/sip/SipManager.html "android") 2.3.4 + built-in - wifi only [sipdroid](http://sipdroid.org/ "sipdroid") + works over wifi and 3g

### voip sip service providers

[callcentric](http://www.callcentric.com/ "callcentric") + free inum [ekiga](http://ekiga.org/ "ekiga") + free STUN server [euteliavoip](http://www.euteliavoip.com/index.php?lang=en "euteliavoip") + free uk numbers [gradwell](http://www.gradwell.com/ "gradwell") + smb & sme focused [ovh](http://www.ovh.co.uk/telephony/ "ovh") + just £1/m uk 01 or 02 number and unlimited uk 01 and 02 calls [sipgate](http://www.sipgate.com/ "sipgate") + free uk 01 or 02 number [voipalot](http://www.voipalot.com/ "voipalot") + clients for androids, mobiles, symbians and pc clients [voipbuster](http://www.voipbuster.com/en/mobilevoip.php "voipbuster") + clients for mobiles and pcs [voipfone](http://www.voipfone.co.uk/ "voipfone") + £1/m for uk 01 or 02 number

### voip sip pc softphones

[ekiga](http://ekiga.org/ "ekiga") + floss linphone + floss twinkle + floss CounterPath [X-lite](http://www.counterpath.com/x-lite.html "x-lite") + mac and pc clients
