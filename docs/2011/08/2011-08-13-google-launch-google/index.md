[goog](http://google.co.uk) have launched their  gchat gtalk gvoice service in the the UK so you can now make VOIP calls to any landline or mobile phone in the world from your browser (with Adobe Flash) running on WXP, WVS, Mac or GNU/Linux !

- [https://www.google.com/voice/b/0/rates?hl=en-GB](https://www.google.com/voice/b/0/rates?hl=en-GB "gvoice")

to get started you'll need use gcheckout to buy a mininum topup of £10+20% VAT

- calls to AUS are 2p/min
- calls to UK 080 numbers are 7p/min
